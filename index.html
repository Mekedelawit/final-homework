About Us

Aleph Hotel is situated in Addis Ababa, an 8-minute walk from Custom Headquarters.The property is a 14-minute walk from a Mall. At the hotel the rooms are fitted with a balcony with a city view. At Aleph Hotel, each room comes with a TV with satellite channels. A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the accommodation. Boundless Ethiopia Tours is 1.3 km from Aleph Hotel.
